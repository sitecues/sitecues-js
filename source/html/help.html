sitecues zoom and speech — help and tips

Zoom or read this website with sitecues truly equal access. You can zoom the entire web page or just part that interests you. You can even listen to the part that interests as it's read aloud.

Page Zoom

With the mouse, hover over the sitecues icon and use the built-in zoom slider to zoom in and out.

Press + or - to zoom with the keyboard — please see the full list of keyboard tips below

One-Touch Read

The One-Touch Read feature zooms content even more or speaks it aloud. One-Touch Read is enabled once either the sitecues zoom or speech feature have been turned on.

Zoom part of a web page

  • Start by moving your mouse to something you want to read. It should be highlighted (if it isn't, make sure either zoom or speech have been enabled in sitecues).
  • Press the spacebar to zoom the highlighted area.

Listen to part of a web page

First, turn on speech:

  • With the mouse, hover over the sitecues icon and click on the speech button
  • Finally, highlight something you want to read, and press the spacebar key.

Keyboard quick commands

The keyboard can be an even faster way to do things:

Zooming the page

  • + to zoom in
  • - to zoom out
  • Hold these keys down longer to zoom or unzoom for longer

One-Touch Zoom and Speech

  • Spacebar to turn on highlighting.
  • Up / Down / Left / Right arrow keys to move around the page.
  • Press Spacebar a second time to One-Touch Read the highlighted area.
  • Alt+S to turn speech on or off
  • Escape to close One-Touch Read or to clear highlighting
