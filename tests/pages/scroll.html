A Short History of Batman

To make a long story short, the history of Batman is basically that of a young boy named Bruce Wayne belonging to a rich family whose parents were murdered during a mugging in front of his eyes. The only things the young Bruce still had left where his enormous inheritance and a loyal and caring butler in Alfred. Throughout the years Bruce Wayne has grown to become one of the most proficient people in just about anything, mastering not only various martial arts, but also investigative techniques and just about any topic they teach you about at university; he was a genius and a wrecking machine.

Throughout his never-ending crusade against the forces of evil, Batman encounters countless deranged maniacs and murderous psychopaths all of whom plot to either take control or simply destroy the city of Gotham, a place where daily terror attacks against innocent civilians is something of a given. Some of his most renowned enemies include the now worldwide famous Joker, the seemingly invincible Bane, the bizarrely seductive Poison Ivy, and Scarecrow who is incredibly close to coming off as a shady drug dealer hanging around schoolyards. It seems that every time Batman takes down some eccentric megalomaniac plotting to destroy his city, another takes his place in the blink of an eye.

At one point, Bruce, being obscenely rich and allegedly bored out of his mind, decided that it was time to rid the city of Gotham from the criminal parasite which seems to have rotten this city to the core. After considering his options, and possibly having a debate with Alfred about his image choice, Bruce Wayne decided that he would don a costume themed after bats, and naturally he would be called the Batman. At his disposal is an arsenal of various custom-made batarangs (sort of like boomerangs, but modeled after a bat), grappling hooks, bombs, and various other accessories all themed after bats of course.
